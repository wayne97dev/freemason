The Creed of the Thirteen

For centuries, the Order has guarded the secret knowledge transmitted by the Founding Fathers. Thirteen are the principles that uphold the Temple. Thirteen the seals of the Master. Thirteen the truths that separate the initiate from the profane.

I

Lux in Tenebris

The Light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not received it. Seek the Light wherever it is hidden.

II

Ordo ab Chao

From the apparent chaos of the world, the initiate discerns the sacred order that governs all things.

III

Trinitas

Three are the pillars of the Temple: Wisdom, Strength, Beauty. Three the sides of the Pyramid.

IV

Quattuor Elementa

Fire, Water, Earth, Air — the four alchemical elements sustain the manifest world.

V

Pentagrammaton

The fifth element, Spirit, animates matter and joins heaven to earth.

VI

Sigillum Salomonis

«As above, so below.» The microcosm reflects the macrocosm.

VII

Septem Artes

The seven liberal arts are the stairways by which the initiate ascends to the higher degrees.

VIII

Octava Sphæra

Beyond the seven ancient planets lies the eighth sphere, dwelling of the fixed stars.

IX

Nona Camera

In the ninth chamber of the Temple is kept the Word that may not be spoken aloud.

X

Decem Sephiroth

The ten divine emanations compose the Tree of Life, the map of the cosmos.

XI

Magnum Opus

The alchemical Great Work transmutes the lead of the soul into the gold of the Spirit.

XII

Duodecim Signa

The twelve signs of the Zodiac mark the initiate's passage through the sacred year.

XIII

Magisterium

The thirteenth degree is the Fulfillment: only those who have walked through all the previous may enter the supreme Mystery.

The Oath

«I, Initiate of the Order, swear by the Eye that sees all, by the Pyramid that touches the heavens, by the eternal Fire that burns in the breast of free men —

to keep the seals, to honor the brethren, never to reveal to the profane what has been transmitted unto me. Sub rosa, in saecula saeculorum.»
